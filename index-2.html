3T Impex Consulting Limited

Who We Are

At 3T Impex Consulting Limited, we are more than just exporters—we are trade enablers, consultants, and educators, dedicated to helping businesses succeed in international trade. Based in Lagos, Nigeria, our company specializes in training businesses in global trade, offering export advisory services, and supplying premium-quality products for international markets. We work with Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) and large corporations, providing them with comprehensive trade education programs, export compliance guidance, financing support, and market entry strategies. Our mission is to increase Africa’s Gross Domestic Product (GDP) through international trade expansion. Our export division supplies a diverse range of high-quality Nigerian agricultural and processed food products, including PAP (Custard), Garri, Roasted Cashew Nuts, Shea Butter, Herbal Teas, Spices, and Processed Flours. We also supply Plastic Scraps, Black Soap, Animal Feeds, and African Children’s Clothing, ensuring ethical and sustainable sourcing practices. We prioritize trade transparency, compliance with international standards, and efficient logistics management to ensure seamless global transactions. With a minimum order quantity of one 20ft container and a maximum of three 20ft containers, we cater to wholesalers, importers, and food processing industries.
