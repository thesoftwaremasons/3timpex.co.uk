3T Impex Consulting Limited

About Us

3T Impex Consulting Limited is a globally recognized trade training and consulting firm, dedicated to fostering international trade and economic growth in Africa. Established in 2010 and headquartered in Surulere, Lagos, Nigeria, we are committed to empowering businesses with the knowledge, tools, and strategies needed for successful export operations. Our core mission revolves around Trade Education, Trade Enhancement, and Trade Execution. We provide comprehensive trade training programs, export readiness assessments, trade financing guidance, compliance support, market entry strategies, and logistics advisory. Through our expertise, we have trained thousands of businesses in international trade and helped hundreds of Nigerian companies establish successful export ventures. In addition to our consulting services, we engage in the export of high-quality agricultural and processed food products, including PAP (Custard), Garri (Cassava Flakes), Roasted Cashew Nuts, Cashew Kernels, Shea Butter, Soybean Oil, Roasted Peanuts, Herbal Teas, Spices Powder, Processed Flours (Beans, Plantain, Yam), Sesame Seeds, Ginger, and Soya Beans. We also supply Plastic Scraps, Black Soap, Animal Feeds (Insect Flours and Meals), and African Children’s Clothing.

"3T Impex Consulting – Your Gateway to Global Trade."
